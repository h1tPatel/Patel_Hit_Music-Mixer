How to Use

1. Drag and Drop Music Files: - Drag music files from the "Music Files" section and drop them into any of the available drop zones to mix the tracks.
2. Drop Zone Restrictions: - Each drop zone can only contain one music file at a time. If you drop a new music file into an occupied drop zone, it will replace the existing one.
3. Play Music: - Once a music file is dropped into a drop zone, click on the drop zone to play the music. You can play multiple music files simultaneously by clicking on different drop zones.
4. Reset Mixer: - Click the "Reset" button to reset the mixer to its initial state. This will move all music files back to the "Music Files" section and pause any currently playing music.
5. Save Your Mix: - Use the "Save" or "Export" button to save your mixed track. You can choose the format in which you want to export your mix (e.g., MP3, WAV).
6. Supported File Formats: - The music mixer supports common audio file formats such as MP3, WAV, and OGG. Ensure that the files you upload are in these formats.
7. Mobile Devices: - The music mixer is optimized for desktop and laptop computers. While it may work on mobile devices, we recommend using a larger screen for better control and visibility.
8. Copyrighted Music: - Ensure you have the necessary rights and permissions to use the music files in the mixer. Respect copyright laws and avoid using copyrighted music without proper authorization.
